# Current Status

## Repo role

Hardware automation and beam-profiling platform for measurement workflows and digital-twin comparison.

## Current state

- The repo remains the hardware-facing counterpart to the more simulation-heavy photonics repos.
- It is responsible for stage control, scan orchestration, measurement analysis, and result export.
- The current direction is to keep acquisition, analysis, and digital-twin workflows tightly integrated.
